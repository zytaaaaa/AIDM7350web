Forest Study Room.

Study room overview

We are committed to creating a comfortable, quiet and natural learning environment for learners. Here, you can get away from the hustle and bustle of the city and devote yourself to your studies. The study room is equipped with modern facilities and comfortable seating to meet your needs for long hours of study.

Features service

Quiet environment: Away from the hustle and bustle of the city, the quiet atmosphere allows you to focus on your studies without outside interference.
Modern amenities: High-speed Internet, comfortable seats and ample charging ports to meet your learning needs.
Provide snacks: Provide snacks so that you can relax your body and mind after studying, replenish energy and relieve study pressure.
