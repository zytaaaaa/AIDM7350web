Details Information

The study room provides a completely private learning environment for learners, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in your studies without any external distractions. Each cabin is equipped with comfortable seats, a spacious desk, and adequate lighting to ensure you feel comfortable and at ease during your study sessions.

The cabin also offers high-speed Wi-Fi and charging ports to meet your electronic device needs. In addition, the excellent soundproofing of the cabin allows you to focus on your studies and enjoy a quiet learning time.

Facilities

  • Comfortable seats and a spacious desk
  • Adequate lighting and good ventilation
  • High-speed Wi-Fi and charging ports
  • High-quality soundproofing facilities
